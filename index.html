About Us

The St Ann's SpotReserve application streamlines booking AV rooms and auditoriums for campus events. It replaces manual processes with an intuitive platform, offering real-time venue availability, secure role-based access, and automated notifications. Key features include an approval workflow, conflict management with alternative suggestions, and a centralized dashboard for administrators to monitor usage and generate reports.
